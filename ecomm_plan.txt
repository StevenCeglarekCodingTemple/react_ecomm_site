for our ECOMM website

example of a user story

As a user
I want to be able to add an item to my cart
Then checkout


A user goes to our ecomm website
they can see all of the products on our home page
They can click on a product from the home page
and get navigated to the product details page
the user can add to cart from product details page
User can go to shopping cart page and checkout from there

(Optional) From product details page, the user can see reviews and ratings of product
